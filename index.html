How Did WWII Start?

World War II began on September 1, 1939, when Germany invaded Poland.

Adolf Hitler

Adolf Hitler was the leader of Nazi Germany. His aggressive expansion led to the war.

Countries Involved

Allied Powers: USA, UK, Soviet Union, China

Axis Powers: Germany, Italy, Japan

Major Battles

  • Battle of Britain
  • Battle of Stalingrad
  • D-Day: Battle of Normandy
  • Battle of Midway

The Holocaust

The Holocaust was the genocide of six million Jews and millions of others by Nazi Germany.

End of the War

The war in Europe ended in May 1945. The war in the Pacific ended after atomic bombs were dropped on Japan in August 1945.

WWII Timeline

  • 1939: Germany invades Poland
  • 1941: Pearl Harbor attack
  • 1944: D-Day invasion
  • 1945: War ends in Europe and Pacific
